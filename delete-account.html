Clear Spending – Account & Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your Clear Spending account and all associated data, you may request deletion at any time by emailing us at:

Email: clearappshelp@gmail.com

What will be deleted?

  • Your Firebase Authentication account (email login)
  • All budget data associated with your account
  • All saved budgets, expenses, and allowance entries
  • Any shared budget data linked to your user ID

What will NOT be deleted?

  • Anonymous crash or technical logs (if any are created by Google Play)

How to request deletion

Send an email to clearappshelp@gmail.com with the subject line:

"Account Deletion Request – Clear Spending"

Include the email address used to create your account.

How long does deletion take?

Data deletion requests are processed within 7 days.

Questions?

If you have any questions, contact us at: clearappshelp@gmail.com

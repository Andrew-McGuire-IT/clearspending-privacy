Clear Spending — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Contact Email: clearappshelp@gmail.com

1. Introduction

Clear Spending (“the App”) is a personal budgeting tool. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Email address (for login via Firebase Authentication)
  • Budget data you manually enter (income, expenses, categories, allowance logs)

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Firebase Authentication data (email only — we do not store your password)
  • Firestore or local encrypted storage for budget data

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and maintain your account
  • To save and sync your budgets
  • To support shared budgets with other users

We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase Authentication, Firestore, or encrypted local storage.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.

7. Your Choices

You may request account deletion by emailing clearappshelp@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at clearappshelp@gmail.com.
